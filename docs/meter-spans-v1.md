# Meter spans v1 — selection-authored local meter

This increment is deliberately preview-first.

## Authoring

1. Select the first through last **note attack** in one notation line.
2. Focus the Meter field and type `3`, `6`, `5/7`, `4/3`, etc.
3. The selected passage highlights in Preview with a dashed lower arch.
4. Press Enter or Apply.
5. Sargam writes a generated companion line and renders a permanent lower arch.

Example generated source:

```text
@8 .D--.n -S gm | D - ~(D m) [[g---RS R-]] S-
>> 4/3 @0..3/2; 2 @3/2..3; 6 @7..8
```

The offsets are exact matras measured from the start of the music line. They are generated by the app; musicians are not expected to calculate them.

## Binding v1 behavior

- Tala, sam, khali, playback, and written durations do not move.
- The meter span declares and validates the local grid already present in the music.
- The first and last selected attacks become the arch boundaries.
- Every attack inside must land on the declared grid.
- Changing a meter does not silently reflow the notation.
- Overlapping spans are replaced by the newly applied span; touching spans are legal.
- Preview renders the meter lane. Export integration is the next increment after the interaction is ear/eye approved.
